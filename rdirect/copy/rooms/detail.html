*PLEASE NOTE, I LIKE TO KNOW A LITTLE ABOUT THE PEOPLE WHO ARE GOING TO STAY IN MY HOME, AND I LIKE TO HAVE A MESSAGE OR TWO BACK AND FORTH BEFORE THE ACTUAL RESERVATION IS MADE. SO PLEASE, FILL IN YOUR PROFILE, AND DON'T CLICK ON "BOOK IT" UNTIL WE HAVE HAD A CHANCE TO COMMUNICATE FIRST.*

SUMMARY IF YOU DON'T WANT TO READ ALL THIS:

BEAUTIFUL / LIGHT / SAFE / CENTRALLY LOCATED / ELEVATOR / DOORMAN / DOUBLE BED + QUEEN AIR BED / WiFi + PREMIUM CABLE / 32" FLAT SCREEN / TINY KITCHEN / SHEETS + TOWELS

This apartment was a finalist in Apartment Therapy's Small, Cool 2010 Contest

Beautiful and light one bedroom next door to the U.N. in Midtown East. On the fifth floor of a historic elevator building with 24 hour doorman.

Beautifully decorated, the apartment features a double bed in the bedroom, plus a queen airbed that can be brought into service if needed. There is a walk-in closet with plenty of hanging space, plus a small chest of drawers for your use. A hair dryer, travel iron, and umbrella are available.

In the living room there is a large, comfortable couch for lounging or TV watching, a small writing table (can be used as a bar for entertaining), a reading chair by the window and a dining table, too. A bookshelf holds a small, diverse collection of books, and I have maps of the city and subway system for you to borrow.

There is an A/C unit, or you can open the windows in nice weather. Warning: this is New York City -- when the windows are open, it is not particularly quiet!

High speed internet, a printer and paper (bring your own laptop), 32" flat screen TV, premium cable channels, DVD player and small library of movies.

Small kitchen mainly suitable for warming up food and fixing drinks. Please look at the picture and judge for yourself whether it will fit your needs if you want to cook a lot -- definitely not made for elaborate cooking. All the basics are there: small stove, oven and fridge, toaster oven and microwave, dishes, utensils, pots and pans. Also, ice bucket, bar tools and wine glasses for entertaining or quiet evenings with a drink.

Fresh, clean sheets and plenty of towels are provided, and there are 6 pillows there, some feather/down and some synthetic. Laundry facilities are in the building, or your laundry can be done for you for an added fee.

There is a 24 hour convenience store directly across the street as well as around the corner. Within two blocks you will find: a 24 hour drug store, cafe/bakery, dry cleaner, fruit stand, health food store, gourmet supermarket, bank, organic hair salon, post office, and much, much more.

There are many wonderful restaurants and take out places within a block, and TONS within 3 blocks.

From the building it is a 6 minute walk to Grand Central, where you can get a subway to almost anywhere. A bus stop is a 2 minute walk in either direction. It's super safe and secure, with a 24 hour doorman, security cameras within the building. There also happens to be a lot of extra security along the street because of proximity to U.N., but even without all that, it's simply a nice, safe area (though I think almost all of Manhattan is nice and safe).

What else can I tell you? Just ask!
- Check in at 2:00 p.m. (or later); check out 12:00 p.m. (sometimes there is flexibility if needed).
- Maximum stay 12 days.
- One time cleaning fee, to be paid separately from the nightly fee, of $100 ($150 for three guests).
- Please check the calendar for availability.
